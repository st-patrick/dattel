Hot Tips

  • Keep your camera off when no one is in your room. Talk for 1 or 2 minutes first, then turn on the camera after.
  • If you use any video meeting service, check what name appears for you. You might not want to let everyone know your full name.
  • Under location, list the nearest larger cities you would be ok meeting in, e.g. "Los Angeles, San Diego". If they are small, don't worry about it, so long as it represents your preferences.
